I'm Lars, a web software developer and consultant from Germany.

I specialize in building and operating web applications with
[Ruby on Rails](https://rubyonrails.org/) and other frameworks, as well as
telephony applications (IVR) built on
[FreeSWITCH](https://freeswitch.com/) and [Asterisk](https://www.asterisk.org/).
Most of my work runs on self-hosted infrastructure using
[Dokku](https://dokku.com/) and [Ansible](https://www.ansible.com/).

This site is a digital garden — a growing, opinionated collection of notes,
learnings, and tools I find useful. Expect posts on Ruby, Rails, AI, CLI tools,
self-hosting, and the occasional tangent.

## Beyond the screen

I volunteer with a local chamber of commerce on IT vocational examination
boards. In my spare time you'll likely find me outdoors — windsurfing,
paddling, biking, hiking, or more recently on a padel court.
